Duo Plus

The Duo™ Plus 9-In-1 multi-use electric pressure cooker has all of the great features that made the Duo the #1 best seller, plus more.

The new Duo Plus* comes with upgraded easy-seal lid and sous vide smart program.

The EasySeal™ lid automatically seals the pot for pressure cooking when you close the lid and has a quick steam release button for ease of use.

The 3 Quart model has 13 one-touch Smart Programs, while the 6 and 8-Quart Duo Plus boast 15: it is a pressure cooker, rice cooker, slow cooker, yogurt maker, steamer, sauté pan, food warmer, stock pot, plus with additional programs it’s also cake maker (6 and 8-Quart only), egg cooker, sterilizer, and sous vide. It’s everything you need to get nutritious dinners on the table quicker.

3 Quart – the mini works great for preparing side dishes or when cooking for just 1 or 2 people (does not have Cake or Beans/Grains)

6 Quart – perfect families of up to 5 people and makes weekly meal prep a breeze

8 Quart – for when you need to feed a crowd of 6 or more, or for large batch prep

In addition to the standard stainless steel exterior, the 6-Quart even comes in several colors to match the décor of any kitchen: black, blue, cinnamon, and copper.
